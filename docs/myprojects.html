My Project

Top Tracks in Spotify (2000 to 2019)

In my project, I will be analysing a dataset containing information of the top tracks in Spotify between the year 2000 to 2019. This dataset was taken from Kaggle, a free dataset sharing platform.

Introduction: An overview of Spotify

Spotify is a digital music, podcast, and video service that provides access to songs and other content from many creators globally (What Is Spotify?, n.d.). It is the world’s most popular audio streaming subscription service with more than 602 million users, including 236 million subscribers in more than 180 markets (Spotify — about Spotify, n.d.).

Why this dataset?

I have been an avid and frequent user of Spotify for quite some time. Over the years, I have developed an interest and habit of listening to top tracks within the streaming platform. Thus, I am keen to analyse the different aspects of top song hits through more in-depth data insights.

This dataset is a csv file that contains audio statistics of the top 2000 tracks on Spotify from 2000-2019. The data contains about 18 columns each describing the track and its qualities.

Main Problem Statement

In my data story, I will be analysing various song properties and their relationships with song popularity.

The main question I wish to answer is “What determines the extent of popularity of songs in Spotify?”
